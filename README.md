## 📌 Overview
This project delivers an **end-to-end business analysis** of KFC sales operations.  
It focuses on extracting actionable insights into **revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and delivery efficiency** using industry-standard analytics tools.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze **sales performance and order trends**  
- Identify **peak demand periods**  
- Evaluate **top and low performing products**  
- Monitor **delivery efficiency and delays**  
- Understand **customer growth and engagement**  
- Track **category-wise revenue contribution**

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🍗 Product Performance
- **Chicken Bucket (8pcs)** is the highest revenue-generating product  
- **Zinger Burger & Chicken Strips** contribute significantly to total sales  
- **Brownie Sundae & Coleslaw** are low-performing items  

---

### 💰 Sales Analysis
- Consistent revenue performance across the period  
- **Weekend sales (₹61M)** outperform weekdays  
- Noticeable spike on **Wednesdays (₹32M)**  

---

### 🚚 Delivery Analysis
- Average delivery time maintained at **~25 minutes**  
- Very low late delivery rate (**1%**)  
👉 Indicates **efficient delivery operations**

---

### 👥 Customer Insights
- Strong and growing customer base  
- High order volume reflects **good customer retention and engagement**

---

### ⏰ Order Pattern Analysis
- Peak demand during:
  - 🌅 Lunch hours  
  - 🌆 Evening hours  
- Stable demand throughout the day
